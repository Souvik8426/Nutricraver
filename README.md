This is a [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) project bootstrapped with [`create-next-app`](https://nextjs.org/docs/app/api-reference/cli/create-next-app).

**NutriCraver is like a smart food-ordering app that combines a Swiggy or Zomato-style experience with a nutrition assistant**.

How a normal user would use it in real life:

Browse restaurants and dishes.
Add items to cart like a regular delivery app.
Chat with the built-in AI to share needs, for example:
“I want high-protein meals”
“I’m allergic to peanuts”
“I want more fiber with dinner”
Tap Smart Recommend in Cart to get healthier pairings and swaps based on what is already in your cart.
Checkout normally.
What makes it useful:

It keeps your cravings intact instead of forcing strict diet food.
It remembers your preferences over time (diet style, allergies, favorite patterns).
It gives practical improvements, like adding fiber sides to rich meals or better carb options.
It adapts suggestions to your location so recommendations feel realistic for nearby food choices.
In simple terms:
It helps you order what you like, but in a smarter and healthier way, without making ordering complicated.
